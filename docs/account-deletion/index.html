Account Deletion

Last updated: 27 May 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from within the HelpHer app. No need to contact us.

  1. Open the app and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section.
  3. Tap "Delete Account" and confirm when prompted.
  4. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

What Happens to Your Data

DataWhat happensTimeframe
Account (email, name, username) Deleted Immediately
Profile photo Deleted Immediately
Community posts Deleted Immediately
Encrypted messages (on our servers) Deleted Immediately
E2EE key backup Deleted Immediately
Push notification token Deleted Immediately
SOS alert history Deleted Within 90 days
Messages already delivered to other users Remains on recipient's device We cannot retrieve content from other users' devices
Emergency contacts Stored locally only — uninstalling the app removes them On uninstall

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access your account to delete it in-app, contact us and we will process the deletion manually within 30 days after verifying account ownership:

github.com/arda-yusuf-yilmaz/HelpHer/issues

Please do not post personal information publicly. We will direct you to a private channel.
